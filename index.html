Introduction

ENR is a major student association at EDHEC Business School. At Edhec North Race, we aim to reunite students from all over France around our passion for water sports such as surfing, kitesurfing, wakeboarding, and many more. ENR started small with only 5 creators and became today one of the biggest association at EDHEC with 40 members. Each year, we organise many events with students from EDHEC and other schools.
